About Me

Ajay Kumar Gupta is an Indian educator and school administrator with over a decade of experience in the field of education. Born on 16 September 1990, he has served in various roles including Vice Principal, Principal, and currently as a Government School Teacher in Bihar.

Throughout his career, Ajay has demonstrated a strong commitment to educational excellence and student development. His journey from private school administration to government teaching reflects his dedication to making quality education accessible to all.

He married on 7 June 2022 and values maintaining a healthy balance between his professional responsibilities and personal life.
